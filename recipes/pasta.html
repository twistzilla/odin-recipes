Easy Homemade Pasta

Some delicious homemade pasta!

Pasta is a wonderful foundation for a number of dishes! It is a combination of flour and eggs, rolled up kneaded into a dough. which is then press and pulled into strands!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make the Dough
  2. Turn the dough out and knead for 10 minutes
  3. Let the Dough Rest
  4. Divide the dough into balls
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough
