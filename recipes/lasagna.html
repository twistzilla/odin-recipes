Lasagna

something of a cheesy dish

Lasagna is a timeless recipe! The beloved dish is made up of primarily meats and cheeses! Stuffed inbetween sheets a pastry dough!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatos
  • Sugar
  • Spice and Season
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
